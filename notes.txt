Q1 . what is database?

A1 . Databases provides a structured and organized way to store data, making it easier to access, query, and update.

Q2 . Types of Databases

A2 . 1.Relational database (SQL) - In Relational Database, Data is stored in a table (also called as relation) having rows & columns.

     2.Non Relational  (Mongo db) - In Non Relational data is not stored in the form of tables or rows or columns but in a key value pair
